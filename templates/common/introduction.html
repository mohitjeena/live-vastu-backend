what is vastu

Vaastu is not belief-based. It is a rule-based science documented in classical texts such as Mayamatam and Vishwakarma Prakāsha.

These texts clearly state that when directions are violated, the house itself becomes the cause of loss, fear, and instability. Prosperity exists only where directional discipline is followed.

Vaastu defects do not act suddenly. They begin as delays, stress, health imbalance, or financial leakage and increase with time if ignored.

At Live Vaastu, under the guidance of Dr. Puneet Chawla, analysis and corrections are done strictly as per Shastra principles. Correction does not change destiny; it removes resistance created by directional imbalance.
